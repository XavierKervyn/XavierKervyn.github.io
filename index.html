Welcome!

I'm Xavier, currently enrolled in the Part III of the Mathematical Tripos (MASt in Applied Mathematics) at the University of Cambridge, UK. I previously completed my undergraduate education in Physics at the Swiss Federal Institutes of Technology of Lausanne (EPFL) and Zurich (ETHZ). I steer my cursus towards the study of quantum gravity, i.e. research at the crossroads of high energy and gravitation theories.

This is my personal webpage. You'll find a description of my current and recent academic activities, as well as various resources which I've found to be worth looking at in my journey so far. Make sure to check these out!
