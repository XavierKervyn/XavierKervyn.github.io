Welcome!

I'm Xavier, currently enrolled in the Part III of the Mathematical Tripos (MASt in Applied Mathematics) at the University of Cambridge, UK. I steer my cursus towards the study of quantum gravity, i.e. research at the crossroads of high energy and gravitation theories. On this website, I share some of my recent academic activities, as well as various resources (lectures, MOOCs, etc.) which I've found to be worth looking at in my journey so far. Check these out if you think that we share common interests!
